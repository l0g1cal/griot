Why it scales

History is free — it's git, deterministic at any size. Rules regeneration is free — a template render, no LLM. The LLM cost is the huddle-queue construction over one day of commits — cost tracks change velocity, not repo size. And the context window is protected: only high-severity knowledge is pushed; the long tail is pulled through MCP.
